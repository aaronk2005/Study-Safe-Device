Instructions

Follow the steps below to get started with your Study Safe device:

  1. Set up your Study Safe device: Connect the device to power and ensure it is turned on. Make sure the accelerometer is properly connected.
  2. Connect the device to Wi-Fi: Follow the on-screen prompts to connect the device to a Wi-Fi network. You'll need to input the network's SSID and password.
  3. Enter your phone number: Input your phone number so that you can receive alerts in case of unauthorized movement. Ensure the number is in the correct format (e.g., +16471234567).
  4. Activate the monitoring system: Once connected, you can toggle between 'Away Mode' and 'With Device Mode'. 'Away Mode' will monitor for unauthorized movement, while 'With Device Mode' will deactivate the alarm.
  5. Receive real-time alerts: If movement is detected in 'Away Mode', an alarm will sound, and you will receive an SMS alert on your phone.
